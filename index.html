Yujian Lee (李宇健)

I am an undergraduate student majoring in Artificial Intelligence, Faculty of Science and Technology, Beijing Normal University - Hong Kong Baptist University United International College, Guangdong Zhuhai.

My research interests include but are not limited to Bioinformatics (Cell-cell interaction network reconstruction), Computer Vision (Cross modal pedestrians re-identification), Data Minning (Efficient and fast clustering algorithm), and AI for Science.

Selected Publications

Paper Code
